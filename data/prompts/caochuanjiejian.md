# Cao Chuan Jie Jian (草船借箭) Prompts

## Scene 1: 公瑾设计 (The Scheme)
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Inside a military tent during the Three Kingdoms period. Zhou Yu sits on the main commander seat, wearing intricate armor and looking commanding with a hidden smirk. Zhuge Liang stands calmly in front of him, holding a feather fan, wearing a Taoist robe. The atmosphere is tense but polite. Ancient Chinese military tent interior, wooden pillars, maps in background. no text --ar 16:9

**中文翻译:**
中国漫画风格。三国时期的军帐内。周瑜坐在主帅位上，身穿精致铠甲，神情威严，暗藏诡计。诸葛亮平静地站在他面前，手持羽扇，身穿道袍。气氛紧张而礼貌。古中国军帐内部，木柱，背景有地图。无文字。

## Scene 2: 私借草船 (The Secret Plan)
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Night time. Zhuge Liang and Lu Su are talking privately in a smaller tent or a quiet corner. Zhuge Liang is whispering requests to Lu Su, looking mysterious. Lu Su looks worried and attentive. Intimate and secretive atmosphere. Candlelight illumination. Ancient Chinese robes. no text --ar 16:9

**中文翻译:**
中国漫画风格。夜间。诸葛亮和鲁肃在小帐篷或安静的角落私下交谈。诸葛亮神神秘秘地向鲁肃耳语请求。鲁肃看起来忧心忡忡，全神贯注。亲密而神秘的气氛。烛光照明。古中国长袍。无文字。

## Scene 3: 雾锁长江 (Fog on the River)
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Heavy fog covering the surface of a wide river (Yangtze River). Several wooden boats connected by iron chains are barely visible in the thick mist. On the bow of one boat, Zhuge Liang and Lu Su sit and drink wine. The view is atmospheric and mysterious, with strong fog effects using white and grey tones. Ancient Chinese wooden warships. no text --ar 16:9

**中文翻译:**
中国漫画风格。大雾笼罩宽阔的江面（长江）。几艘用铁链连接的木船在浓雾中若隐若现。在船头，诸葛亮和鲁肃坐着饮酒。画面充满氛围感和神秘感，利用白色和灰色调表现浓雾效果。古中国木制战船。无文字。

## Scene 4: 曹营惊弓 (Panic in Cao Camp)
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. View from the perspective of Cao Cao's camp looking out at the river. Cao Cao stands on a platform, looking suspicious and commanding. Thousands of archers are lined up, shooting arrows blindly into the thick fog towards the river. A rain of arrows flying into the mist. Dynamic action, tension. Ancient Chinese military camp by the water. no text --ar 16:9

**中文翻译:**
中国漫画风格。从曹操营寨看向江面的视角。曹操站在高台上，神情多疑且威严。成千上万的弓箭手列队，盲目地向浓雾中的江面射箭。箭雨飞入迷雾。动态感，紧张感。古中国水边军营。无文字。

## Scene 5: 谢箭而还 (Returning with Arrows)
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Daytime, fog dispersing. The fleet of straw boats is returning, heavily laden with thousands of arrows stuck into the straw bundles on the sides. Zhuge Liang stands on the boat laughing, soldiers look happy and triumphant. Bright sunlight breaking through the clouds. Victory atmosphere. Ancient Chinese boats with straw targets full of arrows. no text --ar 16:9

**中文翻译:**
中国漫画风格。白天，雾气消散。草船船队归来，船体两侧的草束上密密麻麻地插满了成千上万支箭。诸葛亮站在船上大笑，士兵们看起来高兴且胜利在望。明亮的阳光穿透云层。胜利的气氛。古中国船只，草把上插满了箭。无文字。
