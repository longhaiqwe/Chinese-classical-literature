# San Da Bai Gu Jing Prompts

## Scene 1: 荒山遇美
Chinese Manhua style. Desolate mountain path with overlapping peaks and winding streams. A beautiful young woman with a round face and autumn-wave eyes, wearing green sleeves and a skirt, carrying a green clay pot (basket). She looks gentle and lovely. Sun Wukong looks suspicious with fiery eyes, Pigsy looks happy and eager. No text. --ar 16:9

中国漫画风格。荒凉的山路，峰峦叠嶂，溪流蜿蜒。一位美丽的年轻女子，面如满月，眼如秋波，身穿翠袖湘裙，手提青砂罐（篮子）。她看起来温柔可爱。孙悟空火眼金睛一脸狐疑，猪八戒看起来高兴且急切。无文字。

## Scene 2: 老妇寻女
Chinese Manhua style. Mountain slope. An old woman, 80 years old, leaning on a goosefoot cane, crying and walking step by step. She looks frail and sorrowful. Tang Seng looks angry and guilty, blaming Wukong. Wukong looks furious, recognizing the demon. No text. --ar 16:9

中国漫画风格。山坡。一位80岁的老妇人，拄着藜杖，一步一哭地走着。她看起来虚弱且悲伤。唐僧看起来愤怒且内疚，正在责备悟空。悟空怒目圆睁，认出了是妖精。无文字。

## Scene 3: 老丈寻亲
Chinese Manhua style. An ordinary rural old man, looking anxious and searching for his family, walking towards the group. Sun Wukong stands defensively in front of Tang Seng, blocking the path with his iron staff, looking wary and alert. Tang Seng is behind Wukong. The old man is NOT chanting. No text. --ar 16:9

中国漫画风格。一位普通的乡下老汉，看起来焦急地在寻找家人，正走向师徒一行。孙悟空防御性地挡在唐僧面前，用铁棒挡住去路，看起来警惕且戒备。唐僧在悟空身后。老汉并没有在念佛。无文字。

## Scene 4: 师徒决裂
Chinese Manhua style. Tang Seng is writing a banishment letter (bian shu) and handing it to Wukong, his expression stern and resolute. Wukong is kneeling, looking heartbroken and tearful, begging to stay. White Bone Demon's essence might be lingering invisibly. No text. --ar 16:9

中国漫画风格。唐僧正在写贬书并递给悟空，表情严厉且决绝。悟空跪在地上，看起来心碎流泪，乞求留下。白骨精的妖气可能隐约盘旋。无文字。

## Scene 5: 黑松林遇险
Chinese Manhua style. Inside the Yellow Robed Monster's cave (Moon Water Cave). A dark, eerie atmosphere. A spotted tiger is trapped in a cage (Tang Seng transformed). The Yellow Robed Monster laughs menacingly. Pigsy and Sandy are fighting or defeated. No text. --ar 16:9

中国漫画风格。在黄袍怪的洞府（波月洞）内。黑暗、阴森的气氛。一只斑斓猛虎被困在笼子里（唐僧所变）。黄袍怪狞笑。猪八戒和沙僧正在战斗或已被击败。无文字。

## Scene 6: 义激美猴王
Chinese Manhua style. Flower Fruit Mountain, Water Curtain Cave. Beautiful waterfall and monkeys. Sun Wukong sits on his throne, looking proud but concerned. Pigsy is pleading desperately, trying to goad Wukong into action. No text. --ar 16:9

中国漫画风格。花果山水帘洞。美丽的瀑布和猴群。孙悟空坐在宝座上，看起来骄傲但关切。猪八戒正在拼命恳求，试图用激将法请悟空出山。无文字。

## Scene 7: 破镜重圆
Chinese Manhua style. The Yellow Robed Monster is defeated. Tang Seng is transformed back to human form. Tang Seng looks remorseful and grateful, bowing Wukong's hands. Wukong looks triumphant and comforted. Reunited team. No text. --ar 16:9

中国漫画风格。黄袍怪被击败/擒获。唐僧变回人形。唐僧看起来懊悔且感激，正在行礼或握着悟空的手。悟空看起来胜利且欣慰。师徒重聚。无文字。
