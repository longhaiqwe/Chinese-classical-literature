# Zhen Jia Mei Hou Wang (True and False Monkey King) Prompts

## Scene 1: 被逐蒙冤 (The Wrongful Exile)
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Tang Seng sits weakly by the roadside in a desolate forest, looking disappointed and injured. A fake Sun Wukong, looking identical to the real one but with a sinister smirk, stands arrogantly on a rock at Huaguoshan, surrounded by monkey subordinates. The atmosphere is tense and filled with betrayal. detailed ink lines, muted colors. no text --ar 16:9

**Translation:**
中国硬派漫画风格。唐僧虚弱地坐在荒林路边，神情失望且受了伤。一个假孙悟空，外貌与真悟空无异但带着邪恶的讥笑，傲慢地站在花果山的岩石上，周围环绕着猴子部下。气氛紧张，充满背叛感。细腻的墨线，柔和的色彩。

## Scene 2: 真假莫辨 (Indistinguishable Battle)
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Two Sun Wukongs fighting fiercely in mid-air above Huaguoshan. They are identical in appearance, wearing yellow clothes and tiger-skin skirts, wielding Golden Cudgels. Golden sparks and shockwaves radiate from their clashing weapons. Clouds swirl around them violently. Dynamic action pose, intense combat, high contrast. no text --ar 16:9

**Translation:**
中国硬派漫画风格。两个孙悟空在花果山上空激烈搏斗。他们外貌一模一样，身穿黄衣虎皮裙，手持金箍棒。兵器相撞激发出金色的火花和冲击波。云层在他们周围剧烈翻滚。动态的动作姿势，激烈的战斗，高对比度。

## Scene 3: 南海难分 (Confusion at the South Sea)
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Mount Luojia, a serene bamboo grove with purple mist. Guanyin Bodhisattva sits on a lotus throne, looking puzzled and observing two identical Sun Wukongs standing before her. Both Wukongs look anxious and are pleading their case. White parrot flying nearby. Mystical atmosphere, spiritual lighting. no text --ar 16:9

**Translation:**
中国硬派漫画风格。落伽山，幽静的竹林，紫雾缭绕。观音菩萨坐在莲花座上，面露困惑，注视着面前站着的两个一模一样的孙悟空。两个悟空都显得焦急，正在陈情。白鹦哥在附近飞翔。神秘的氛围，灵性的光线。

## Scene 4: 地府谛听 (Di Ting Listens)
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. The dark and eerie underworld (Diyu). The mythical beast Di Ting lies on the ground with its ear pressed to the floor, listening carefully. The Ten Kings of Hell stand in the background, looking fearful. The two Wukongs stand impatiently nearby. Di Ting has a knowing but terrified expression. Dark shadows, eerie green and blue lighting. no text --ar 16:9

**Translation:**
中国硬派漫画风格。阴森恐怖的地府。神兽谛听趴在地上，耳朵贴着地面仔细聆听。十殿阎王站在背景中，面露惧色。两个悟空不耐烦地站在旁边。谛听露出知晓真相但恐惧的神情。暗影，诡异的蓝绿光效。

## Scene 5: 雷音辨妖 (Revelation at Thunder Monastery)
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. The grand Great Thunder Monastery in the West. Buddha sits majestically on a golden lotus throne, radiating divine golden light. He points a finger at one of the Monkey Kings, who looks panicked and is starting to reveal a six-eared macaque form. The other Wukong looks vindicated. Arhats surround the hall. Epic scale, divine atmosphere. no text --ar 16:9

**Translation:**
中国硬派漫画风格。西天宏伟的大雷音寺。如来佛祖庄严地坐在金莲座上，散发着神圣的金光。他伸指指向其中一个美猴王，那猴王神色惊慌，隐约显露出六耳猕猴的原形。另一个悟空显得沉冤得雪。罗汉们环绕大殿。史诗般的宏大感，神圣的氛围。

## Ending 1 (Success): 师徒重归 (Reunion)
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. A warm and emotional scene on a dusty road. Sun Wukong kneels before Tang Seng, who is helping him up with a regretful and kind expression. The setting sun casts a warm, hopeful glow. Pigsy and Sandy look on happily in the background. Theme of forgiveness and unity. Soft lighting, detailed textures. no text --ar 16:9

**Translation:**
中国硬派漫画风格。尘土飞扬的路上温馨感人的一幕。孙悟空跪在唐僧面前，唐僧带着愧疚和慈悲的神情扶起他。夕阳投下温暖、充满希望的光辉。八戒和沙僧在背景中高兴地看着。宽恕与团结的主题。柔和的光线，细腻的纹理。

## Ending 2 (Failure): 六耳得逞 (Six Ears Prevails)
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. A chaotic and dark version of the journey. The Fake Wukong (Six Eared Macaque) stands triumphantly on a pile of ruins, laughing maniacally towards the sky. The sky is dark and stormy with red lightning. Tang Seng and others are nowhere to be seen. A sense of doom and destruction. Dark fantasy, ominous mood. no text --ar 16:9

**Translation:**
中国硬派漫画风格。取经路上混乱黑暗的版本。假悟空（六耳猕猴）胜利地站在废墟堆上，对着天空疯狂大笑。天空黑暗，风暴肆虐，红色闪电划过。唐僧等人不见踪影。毁灭与末日的̈感觉。黑暗奇幻，不祥的氛围。
