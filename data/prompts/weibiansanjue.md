# 韦编三绝 (Wei Bian San Jue) Image Prompts

**Style Reference:**Style**: Gritty Chinese Manhua style, heavy ink lines, muted colors with splashes of red/gold, dramatic lighting, historic atmosphere.
**Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (Cinematic Widescreen)ing and Autumn period historical setting. Muted earth tones with strategic highlights.

**Character Reference:**
- **Confucius (Kong Zi):** Elderly man (60s-70s), long white beard, wearing traditional Hanfu (scholar robes), looking wise, gentle but determined.

## 1. 晚年喜易 (Reading with Interest)
**Filename:** `bg_01_reading.jpg`
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. A medium shot of elderly Confucius sitting on a woven mat in a simple, ancient Chinese study room. He is holding a roll of bamboo slips (ancient book) with both hands, reading it with intense focus and a slight smile of discovery. The room is quiet, sunlight streaming through a lattice window, illuminating floating dust motes. Shelves with more scrolls in the background. Manhua style, detailed ink lines, bold strokes, cinematic lighting.

## 2. 反复研读 (Studying at Night)
**Filename:** `bg_02_studying.jpg`
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Night scene. Close up on Confucius's face, illuminated by the warm glow of an oil lamp or candle. He looks thoughtful, brows slightly furrowed in deep concentration. He is holding a bamboo slip scroll, perhaps pointing at a character. The background is dark, emphasizing his solitude and dedication. The atmosphere is serene and scholarly. Heavy ink lines, high contrast.

## 3. 韦编断绝 (Broken Leather Strap)
**Filename:** `bg_03_broken.jpg`
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. A dynamic shot showing the bamboo slips of a scroll coming apart. Confucius is holding the scroll, and the leather strap (Wei) has just snapped. Bamboo strips are cascading or scattering slightly onto his lap and the floor. Confucius has a look of mild surprise but calmness, not frustration. Detail on the frayed leather texture. Dramatic composition, bold outlines.

## 4. 三次断绝 (Three Times Broken - Montage)
**Filename:** `bg_04_three_times.jpg`
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. A conceptual montage split or blended image. On one side, Confucius is diligently re-tying the leather straps of the bamboo scroll. On the other side, a pile of worn-out leather straps lies on the table. The bamboo slips look worn and polished from frequent handling. Emphasize the passage of time and the physical wear on the book objects, symbolizing hard work. Atmospheric, textured ink style.

## 5. 自谦之语 (Humble Reflection)
**Filename:** `bg_05_humble.jpg`
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Confucius standing on a porch or near a window, looking out at a twilight sky or a distant landscape. He holds the re-bound scroll by his side. His expression is humble and contemplative, as if communicating with the heavens or reflecting on his life's limitations. A sense of vastness and wisdom. Muted colors, splashes of gold/red, historic feel.

## 6. 千古流芳 (Legacy)
**Filename:** `bg_06_legacy.jpg`
**Prompt:**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Confucius sitting in the center, teaching a group of disciples (Yan Hui, Zi Lu, etc.). In the background, there is a large, artistic, semi-transparent overlay of Hexagram symbols (Bagua) or ancient Chinese text from the Yi Jing, glowing faintly. The scene represents the transmission of knowledge. Warm, hopeful lighting. Epic composition, traditional art influence.
