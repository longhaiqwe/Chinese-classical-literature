# 颜回偷粥 (Yan Hui Steals Porridge) Image Prompts

## 0. 角色设定 (Character Design)
**孔子 (Confucius)**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Character sheet. Confucius (approx 60 years old). Tall, broad-shouldered but currently gaunt from hunger. Long grey beard, wise and deep eyes showing resilience. Wearing worn, dusty, layered Hanfu robes (dark grey and brown). Dignified, sage-like aura despite the poverty. Neutral background. --ar 1:1

**颜回 (Yan Hui)**
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Character sheet. Yan Hui (approx 30 years old). Slender, scholarly, looking malnourished/pale. Clean-shaven or slight goatee, gentle and humble expression. Wearing simple, patched light blue or beige robes. Holding a bamboo scroll. Calm, virtuous demeanor. Neutral background. --ar 1:1

Steam rises from the pot, obscuring parts of his face. The setting is humble and dusty. Warm orange glow from the fire contrasts with the cool, blue shadows of the surrounding poverty. Loose ink strokes. --ar 16:9

Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Close-up action shot. A clump of dust and soot falls from the rotting ceiling directly into the boiling porridge. Yan Hui looks shocked and concerned, holding a wooden ladle to scoop it out. Dramatic lighting highlights the falling debris and his expression. Detailed particles and dynamic composition. --ar 16:9

## 4. 孔子试探 (Confucius's Test)
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Over-the-shoulder shot. Confucius, having seen the act from a distance (blurred in background), tests Yan Hui by suggesting they offer the food to ancestors. Yan Hui stands respectfully, holding the bowl, ready to explain. Tension in the air. Traditional Chinese architecture details. --ar 16:9

## 5. 误会冰释 (Resolution)
Gritty Chinese Manhua style. Two-shot. Confucius places a hand on Yan Hui's shoulder, looking relieved and proud after hearing the truth. Sun streams through a broken window, illuminating them both in a holy, golden light. Peaceful, harmonious atmosphere. Soft ink wash background. --ar 16:9

## 1. 绝粮陈蔡 (陈蔡之困)

粗砺中国漫画风格。广角镜头。饥荒时期，孔子与弟子们端坐在破败的古庙中。众人形容枯槁、面色憔悴，身着磨损的儒袍却保持着庄重仪态。画面弥漫着绝望的沉重感，又被一缕希望的微光点亮。粗粝质感，以大地色系为主，辅以浓重阴影。--ar 16:9

## 2. 颜回讨米 (颜回得米)

粗砺中国漫画风格。仰视视角。瘦削而坚毅的颜回正在角落的简易炉灶前熬粥。蒸腾的热气模糊了他部分面容，环境简陋布满尘埃。灶火的橘色暖光与周遭贫困的冷蓝阴影形成强烈对比。写意水墨笔触。--ar 16:9

## 3. 意外发生 (突发事件)

粗砺中国漫画风格。特写动作镜头。腐朽的房梁突然掉落一团尘灰，正入沸腾的粥锅。颜回震惊忧心地握着木勺欲舀出杂质。戏剧性光影突显下坠的碎屑与他的表情。细腻的粒子效果与动态构图。--ar 16:9

## 4. 孔子试探 (夫子试徒)

粗砺中国漫画风格。过肩镜头。孔子在远处目睹此事（背景虚化），以"祭祖"为由试探颜回。颜回恭敬捧碗而立，正欲解释。空气中弥漫紧张感。传统中式建筑细节。--ar 16:9

## 5. 误会冰释 (冰释前嫌)

粗砺中国漫画风格。双人镜头。听闻真相后，孔子将手搭在颜回肩头，神情欣慰自豪。阳光从残窗倾泻，为二人镀上圣洁金辉。宁静祥和氛围，背景采用柔和泼墨效果。--ar 16:9
